Biography
Hello,

I'm one the NBNU™ Founders; as well as a legal analyst, legal Investigator, civil litigations mediator & legal consultant.

Kimbly Arnold is my name.

I have a team here at the New Black Nation United, of legal aid providers who have undertaken a variety of process to help consumers and self litigants who seeking out our assistance in order to represent themselves in court proceedings.

As law students here, we are also engaged in joint projects with the court of our jurisdisction, attorneys, we sit as co-chair to assist pro se litigants with appropriately preparing pleadings and knowing what to do to pursue or defend their complaint.

​ Some forms of assistance to pro se litigants involve only providing information about the procedural and substantive requirements to pursue or defend complaint.

Our approach to assist pro se litigants involve various forms of limited representation, as NON- lawyers helping our clients, self represent litigants, with discrete pleading process and preparation conducting an investigation to reach a solution.

We urge you to let us help you resolve any and all dispute that you may be facing. Let us save you time, effort and trouble talking to companies who can be of little help or who can care even less.

We strive to protect you as your express legal aid providers, and we work to help you protect what matters to you the most, while making you our top priority.
